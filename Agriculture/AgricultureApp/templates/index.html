Abstract:- Traditional traceability system has problems of centralized management, opaque information, untrustworthy data, and easy generation of information. To solve the above problems, we are designing a traceability system based on blockchain technology for storage and query of product information in supply chain of agricultural products. Leveraging the characteristics of decentralization, tamper-proof. In addition, we design a reputation-based smart contract to network nodes to upload traceability data.

Ethereum is a decentralized, open-source blockchain platform that allows developers to build and deploy smart contracts and decentralized applications Furthermore, we provide performance analysis and practical application, the results show that our system improves the query efficiency and the security of private information, the authenticity and reliability of data in supply chain management in agriculture.
